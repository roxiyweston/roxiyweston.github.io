ABOUT ME
My name is Roxanne Robinson. I grew up in Spanish Fork, Utah. I have worked all over Utah and adore the outdoors of our beautiful state. I worked and lived mainly in Utah County until I got married in 2011 and lived in Riverton, Utah. I had my first child, We moved to Clinton, Utah I had my second son in 2019. I got divorced in 2021 and moved back to Payson in Utah county. I had missed the moutains, fields, and home town feel that I grew up in, and am so happy my kids get to grow up in the Country.

I have been working in customer service for over 25 years. And now Im learning how to apply those skills in a new field.

My first love was medicine. I had my CNA at the age of 17, and worked on an Diabetes type 3 (Alzheimers) unit. I quickly fell in love with my patients and their families.
